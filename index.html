Anthony's Amazing Adventure

One day, Anthony was mindlessly walking on the street when he was suddenly strangled by bandits and held captive in a mysterious prison. The bandits keep watch of him in the daytime, but at night the bandits leave to strangle other people.
Your job is to get Anthony out of the prison before daybreak.

Keep track of how much time you are taking to complete the entire mission.
Try to complete it as fast as possible, as it will determine Anthony&aposs fate! You can also do it at your own pace, and see if you can escape.

Anthony awakes from deep slumber and wanders out of his cell just to find three elevators. Last time he checked, he was roughly 54 kg. Each elevator is held by different ropes and has different masses, but they are currently held at rest:

  • When moving, Elevator 1 has a mass of 980 kg and has a force tension of 9880 N.
  • When moving, Elevator 2 has a mass of 1000 kg and has a force tension of 115000 N.
  • When moving, Elevator 3 has a mass of 1130 kg and has a force tension of 11000 N.
